Gods

This is a list of the most commonly worshipped gods in the campaign world.

Blibdoolpoolp, the goddess of the kuo-toas, is neutral evil. Her titles include the Sea Mother, the Drowning Goddess, and the Whip of Whips. She is worshipped by kuo-toas as well as many other underwater creatures. Her granted domains are Darkness, Death, Destruction, Evil, Madness, Void, and Water.

Callarduran Smoothhands, the god of the gnomes, is true neutral. His titles include the Deep Brother, the Master of Stone, and the Lord of the Deepearth. He is the main god worshipped by the gnomes, though there are some other gods in the svirfneblin pantheon. His granted domains are Artifice, Community, Earth, Healing, Knowledge, Protection, and Trickery.

Deep Duerra, the dwarven demi-goddess of psionics and war, is lawful evil. Her titles include the Queen of the Invisibile Art and the Axe Princess of Conquest. She is worshipped by duergar warriors and psionic duergar as well as many other psionic creatures. Her granted domains are Evil, Law, Psionics, Strength, and War.

Diirinka, the mysterious god of the derro, is chaotic evil. He is known as Diirinka the Betrayer, Diirinka the Father, and the Insane One. His granted domains are Chaos, Evil, Knowledge, Madness, Magic, Psionics, and Trickery.

Eilistraee, the drow demi-goddess of beauty and song, is chaotic good. Her titles include the Dark Maiden, the Lady of the Dance, and Lady Silverhair. She is the daughter of Lolth and as such her worship is not considered heretical in drow society, but worshippers of Eilistraee are routinely persecuted and excluded from positions of power. Her granted domains are Chaos, Charm, Good, Liberation, and Travel.

Ghaunadaur, the god of oozes, slimes, abominations, outcasts, and rebels, is chaotic evil. He is known as That Which Lurks, the Elder Eye, the Lord of Slime, and the Ancient One. He is worshipped by some drow and aboleths as well as various strange monsters. His granted domains are Chaos, Destruction, Earth, Evil, Liberation, Madness, and Void.

Gruumsh, chief god of the orcs, is chaotic evil. His titles are One Eye and He Who Never Sleeps. Gruumsh calls on his followers to be strong and cull the weak from their numbers, and to take all the territory that Gruumsh thinks is rightfully theirs (which is almost everything). His granted domains are Artifice, Chaos, Destruction, Earth, Evil, Strength, and War.

Hruggek, the demi-god of bugbears and exarch of Thanatos, is chaotic evil. He is known as the Decapitator and the Master of Ambush. In addition to bugbears, he is worshipped by some assassins and executioners. His granted domains are Chaos, Death, Evil, Trickery, and War.

Ibrandul, god of caves and tunnels, is chaotic neutral. His titles are Lord of the Dry Depths, the Skulking God, and Lurker in Darkness. He is often worshipped by travelling merchants, adventurers, and others who wander through the caves of the world. His granted domains are Chaos, Darkness, Earth, Luck, Protection, Scalykind, and Travel.

Kiaransalee, the drow demi-goddess of vengeance and undead, is chaotic evil. She is known as the Lady of the Dead, the Revenancer, and the Vengeful Banshee. As part of the drow pantheon, she is worshipped alongside Lolth by drow necromancers, undead drow, and any drow who desires vengeance for past slights. Her granted domains are Chaos, Death, Destruction, Evil, and Nobility.

Kurtulmak, chief demi-god of the kobolds and vassal of Tiamat, is lawful evil. His titles are the Shining One, Gnomesmasher, Steelscale, and the Horned Sorcerer. His granted domains are Evil, Law, Luck, Scalykind, and Trickery.

Laduguer, god of toil and patron of the duergar, is lawful evil. He is known as the Gray Protector, the Master of Crafts, the Slave Driver, the Taskmaster, and the Harsh.  His dogma concerns strict obedience to superiors, empowerment through the exploitation of slaves, and enrichment through mining, craftsmanship, and conquest.  His granted domains are Artifice, Earth, Evil, Law, Magic, Protection, and Rune.

Lolth, the goddess of the drow, is chaotic evil. Her titles include Queen of Spiders, Dark All-Mother, Demon Queen of the Abyss, Weaver of Chaos, and Mother of Lusts. She is worshipped by almost all drow as well as drow-related creatures such as driders and chitines. Her granted domains are Chaos, Death, Evil, Darkness, Destruction, Spider, and Trickery.

Luthic, the orc demi-goddess of caves and mate of Gruumsh, is lawful evil. She is known as the Cave Mother and the Blood Moon Witch.  Her granted domains are Community, Earth, Evil, Healing, and Law.

Maglubiyet, chief god of the goblins and hobgoblins, is lawful evil. His titles are the High Chieftan, Fiery-Eyes, the Mighty One, and the Battle Lord. His granted domains are Destruction, Earth, Evil, Fire, Law, Trickery, and War.

Semuanya, the chief lizardfolk demi-god, is true neutral. He is known as the Survivor, the Scaly One, and the Lizard God.  His granted domains are Animal, Earth, Healing, Protection, and Scalykind.

St. Cuthbert, god of law and justice, is lawful good. His titles are the Blind God, the Maimed God, or the Hand of Justice. His dogma concerns the protection of the innocent, punishment of wrong-doers, and the preservation of good in the world.  His granted domains are Glory, Good, Healing, Knowledge, Law, Protection, and War.

Thanatos, the god of death, is neutral evil. He is known as the Grim Reaper, the Hater of Life, the Bringer of Darkness, and the King of All Gloom. His dogma concerns the creation of undead, the triumph of entropy, and the destruction of all life. His granted domains are Darkness, Death, Destruction, Erosion, Evil, Ruins, and Void.

Tiamat, goddess of evil dragons, is lawful evil. Her titles are the Avaricious, the Dragon Queen, the Great Dragon, and the Prime Evil. She is worshipped by all evil dragons and related creatures, as well as dragon-cults and some kobolds. Her granted domains are Destruction, Evil, Fire, Law, Magic, Strength, Scalykind.

Vhaeraun, the drow demi-god of shadow magic, is chaotic evil. He is known as the Masked Lord, the Shadow Lord, and the Masked Mage. As part of the drow pantheon, he is worshipped alongside Lolth by drow, particularly drow males and arcane magic-users. His granted domains are Chaos, Evil, Darkness, Magic, and Trickery.
