Spider Domain

You share a deep connection with spiders.

Granted Powers:

Familiar: You gain a scarlet spider familiar.  Your effective wizard level for this ability is equal to your cleric level. Your cleric level stacks with levels from other classes that grant familiars when determining the powers of your familiar.

Spider Summoner: You gain Spider Summoner as a bonus feat.

Command Spiders (Su): You can use your channel negative energy to command spiders within 30 ft.  This works just like the Command Undead feat, but applies to spiders instead of undead.

Domain Spells: 1st—spider climb, 2nd—vomit spider swarm, 3rd—phantom spider, 4th—giant vermin, 5th—swarm skin (spider only), 6th—spider curse, 7th—creeping doom, 8th—summon retriever, 9th—summon leng spider
