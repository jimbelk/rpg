Lightstones

Lightstones are minor artifacts that radiate light and positive energy.  Lightstones can be as small as a grain of sand or as large as hundreds of miles across, but a typical lightstone embedded in a magic item has the shape of a flat circle or oval less than an inch across.

Lightstone Properties

In addition to its other properties, all lightstones shed dim light in a 5-foot radius. This light is not affected by any form of magical darkness.  Multiple small lightstones cannot increase the light level beyond dim, but larger lightstones shed much brighter light.

As with all artifacts, lightstones are extremely difficult to damage or destroy. However, unlike most artifacts, lightstones are common enough that they can be bought and sold at most major cities.

Mounting Lightstones

A lightstone can be embedded into any piece of mundane or magical gear that occupies a body slot, including armor, shields, rings, and so forth.  When worn, such an item grants its wearer a +1 bonus to hit points, in addition to other possible benefits. A single item cannot effectively have more than one lightstone mounted on it.

Lightstone Powers

Duergar psions have discovered that certain lightstones possess additional magical or psionic powers. Such powers may be present in any lightstone, but can only be used by the wearer of a headband of light. A given lightstone possesses at most one lightstone power.

Most lightstone powers are activated using a command thought, in a similar way to a psionic item. Unless otherwise noted, this is a standard action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity.

Headband of Light

Aura strong abjuration and conjuration, as well as a faint psionic aura; ML 17th
Slot headband; Price 4,000 gp; Weight 1 lb.

Description This simple silver headband is inlaid with a ring of tiny lightstones. It allows the wearer to use the powers of lightstones embedded in worn items, in addition to the +1 hit point bonus normally granted by a lightstone.

A headband of light cannot itself have any lightstones embedded in it beyond those used in its construction.

Construction Requirements Lightstone Crafting, uses several tiny lightstones; Cost 2,000 gp

Lightstone (Common)

Aura strong abjuration and conjuration; CL 20th
Slot any (inlaid on another item); Price 500 gp; Weight –

Description A common lightstone has no additional powers. It grants the wearer a +1 bonus to hit points, and sheds dim light in a 5-foot radius.

Lightstone of Dimension Hopping

Aura strong abjuration and conjuration; CL 20th
Slot any (inlaid on another item); Price 6,000 gp; Weight –

Description Once per day on command, the wearer of this lightstone can instantly transfer himself from his current location to any other spot within 40 feet to which he has line of sight. The wearer can bring along objects as long as their weight does not exceed the wearer's maximum load.

Lightstone of Dimension Shifting

Aura strong abjuration and conjuration; CL 20th
Slot any (inlaid on another item); Price 10,000 gp; Weight –

Description Once per day on command, the wearer of this lightstone can instantly transfer himself from his current location to any other spot within 500 feet. This works just like a dimension door spell, except that the wearer cannot bring along any other creatures.

Lightstone of Enhancement

Aura strong abjuration and conjuration; CL 20th
Slot any (inlaid on another item); Price 8,000 gp; Weight –

Description This lightstone comes in six different types, each of which grants the wearer a +2 enhancement bonus to a single ability score.

Lightstone of Entanglement

Aura strong abjuration and conjuration; CL 20th
Slot any (inlaid on another item); Price 3,000 gp; Weight –

Description Once per day as a swift action, the wearer of this lightstone can cause one creature that he strikes in melee to become entangled by strands of light. This effect lasts for 1 round, and during that time the target of the effect sheds light similar to that of a lightstone of illumination.

Lightstone of Flight

Aura strong abjuration and conjuration; CL 20th
Slot any (inlaid on another item); Price 6,000 gp; Weight –

Description Once per day on command, the wearer of this lightstone can fill himself with heavenly light. This lets the wearer fly for up to 5 minutes, as though affected by a fly spell. During this time, the wearer sheds light similar to that of a lightstone of illumination.

Lightstone of Healing

Aura strong abjuration and conjuration; CL 20th
Slot any (inlaid on another item); Price 6,000 gp; Weight –

Description Once per day as a swift action, the wearer of this lightstone immediately heals 4d8+7 hit points worth of damage.

Lightstone of Illumination

Aura strong abjuration and conjuration; CL 20th
Slot any (inlaid on another item); Price 1,000 gp; Weight –

Description On command, this lightstone sheds normal light in a 30-foot radius and increases the light level by one step for an additional 30 feet beyond that area (darkness becomes dim light and dim light becomes normal light). This light is not affected by any form of magical darkness. Another command reduces the light to that normally shed by a lightstone.

Lightstone of Lifekeeping

Aura strong abjuration and conjuration; CL 20th
Slot any (inlaid on another item); Price 1,000 gp; Weight –

Description This lightstone grants its wearer a +3 competence bonus on saving throws against energy drain attacks, inflict spells, death spells, and death effects.

Lightstone of Resolve

Aura strong abjuration and conjuration; CL 20th
Slot any (inlaid on another item); Price 4,000 gp; Weight –

Description This lightstone grants its wearer a +3 competence bonus on saving throws against mind-affecting spells and abilities. 

Lightstone of Restoration

Aura strong abjuration and conjuration; CL 20th
Slot any (inlaid on another item); Price 2,500 gp; Weight –

Description Once per day on command, the wearer of this lightstone can heal 1d4 points of temporary ability damage to one of his ability scores

Lightstone of Smiting

Aura strong abjuration and conjuration; CL 20th
Slot any (inlaid on another item); Price 3,000 gp; Weight –

Description Once per day as a swift action, the wearer of this lightstone can imbue all of his attacks with radiant energy for a short time. Until the beginning of the wearer's next turn, all of the wearer's melee attacks deal double damage. During this time, the wearer sheds light similar to that of a lightstone of illumination.

Lightstone of Stamina

Aura strong abjuration and conjuration; CL 20th
Slot any (inlaid on another item); Price 1,000 gp; Weight –

Description This lightstone grants its wearer a +3 competence bonus on saving throws against disease and poison.
