1. Dwarven Armor

All metal armor and shields for sale in Gracklstugh cost 20% less than the usual market price.  Special materials such as mithral and adamantine are available, as are most types of magic armor and psionic armor.

In addition, there one a new type of masterwork item available.

Dwarvencraft Armor

Price +400 gp; Weight —

Description The dwarves have perfected the art of forging masterwork armor. Dwarvencraft armor is treated as masterwork, except that its armor check penalty is reduced by 2 instead of 1. Only armor and shields made from metal or stone can benefit from this ability. The listed price includes the usual cost of masterwork armor, but not the 20% discount on armor in Gracklstugh.

Easy Travel

Aura faint psychometabolism; ML 5th;
Price +1,500 gp; Weight —

Description Easy travel armor seems to weigh less than it should. When wearing armor that has this property, you can carry up to a medium load as if it were a light load (ignoring the maximum Dexterity bonus, check penalty, and reduced speed normally incurred by a medium load). These reductions apply only to penalties for the load you carry, not to any reduction in speed caused by the armor itself.

In addition, you can walk for up to 10 hours in a day before having to make Constitution checks to avoid taking nonlethal damage.

Ring of Enlargement

Aura moderate psychometabolism; ML 7th
Slot ring; Price 3,000 gp; Weight —

Description On command, the wearer of this simple stone ring can cause themselves to grow larger, as the expansion power. This effect lasts for 10 minutes or until you command it to end. A ring of enlargement is usable once per day.

Ring of Escaping

Aura moderate transmutation; CL 7th
Slot ring; Price 3,100 gp; Weight —

Description Crafted by drow wizards, this black metal ring is extremely smooth.  When worn, it fits snugly, though you cannot feel it.  This ring grants the wearer a +5 bonus to Escape Artist checks. In addition, once per day as a swift action, you gain a fly speed of 60 feet with good maneuverability for 1 round.

This ring is not subject to the usual 20% reduction in market price for magic rings in Gracklstugh.

Ring of Mystic Healing

Aura faint conjuration; CL 5th
Slot ring; Price 3,500 gp; Weight —

Description This steel ring glimmers with a faint golden light. A ring of mystic healing provides a boost to your healing spells. When casting cure spells, you gain a +1 competence bonus to your caster level. This is a continuous effect and requires no activation. In addition, this ring has 3 charges per day. As a swift action, you can spend 1 or more charges to grant a bonus to the damage healed by the next cure spell you cast before the end of your turn.
     1 charge: +2d6 points of healing.
     2 charges: +3d6 points of healing.
     3 charges: +4d6 points of healing.

Ring of the Darkhidden

Aura faint psychometabolism; ML 3rd
Slot ring; Price 2,000 gp; Weight —

Description More than a dozen etchings of closed eyes circle this tarnished silver ring. This ring makes you invisible to darkvision, although you are prefectly visible in normal light.

Ring of Vanishing

Aura moderate psychometabolism; ML 7th
Slot ring; Price 2,500 gp; Weight —

Description A ring of vanishing allows you and nearby allies to briefly disappear from sight. The ring has 3 charges per day. On command, you can spend 1 or more charges to turn yourself (and perhaps one or more allies) invisible, as the invisibility spell, for 1 or more rounds.
     1 charge: You become invisible for 4 rounds.
     2 charges: You and one adjacent ally become invisible for 3 rounds.
     3 charges: You and up to three adjacent allies become invisible for 2 rounds.

 

4. Wondrous Items

There is a 20% discount on all gauntlets, helms, pauldrons, armbands, bracers, and vambraces made of metal sold in Gracklstugh. You may assume that such an item is made of metal unless the description of the magic item explicity states otherwise.

Most of the standard wondrous items are available, as are most universal psionic items. The following new wondrous items are also available. The listed prices do not take the 20% discount into account.

Amulet of Fortune Prevailing

Aura moderate abjuration; CL 9th
Slot neck; Price 5,000 gp; Weight 1 lb.

Description This delicate gold amulet is suspended from a fine gold chain and set with three aventurines. Once per day as an immediate action, you can activate an amulet of fortune prevailing to reroll a saving throw. You must activate this ability before the success or failure of the saving throw has been determined, and you must use the second result, even if it’s lower. You can’t use this ability if you have already rerolled the save for any reason.

Belt of One Mighty Blow

Aura moderate abjuration; CL 5th
Slot belt; Price 1,500 gp; Weight 1 lb.

Description The buckle of this wide leather belt is set with a single blood-red carbuncle. Once per day as a swift action, you can use this belt to add extra damage on your next melee attack made before the end of your turn. You must declare that you are using this power before the attack roll is made.  If the attack is successful, you deal 1d8 extra points of damage with a light weapon, 2d6 extra points of damage with a one-handed weapon, or 3d6 extra points of damage with a two-handed weapon.

Bracers of Blinding Strike

Aura strong psychokinesis; ML 12th
Slot wrists; Price 5,000 gp; Weight 1 lb.

Description These elegant bracers are crafted from leather and mithral, and glimmer in the torchlight. Wearing these bracers grants you a +2 competence bonus on initiative checks. This is a continuous effect and requires no activation.

Three times per day as a swift action, you can activate bracers of blinding strike to make one additional attack with any weapon you are holding, as long as you have already used the full attack action during the turn. This attack is made at your full base attack bonus, plus any modifiers appropriate to the situation. This effect is not cumulative with any other effect that grants you an extra attack when making a full attack, such as the Rapid Shot feat, a speed weapon, or the haste spell.

Bracers of Quick Strike

Aura faint psychokinesis; ML 5th
Slot wrists; Price 1,400 gp; Weight 1 lb.

Description Each of these mithral bracers bears an image of a needle-sharp dagger. Once per day as a swift action, you can use the bracers of quick strike to make one extra attack with any weapon you are holding if you already made a full attack on this turn. This attack is made at your full base attack bonus, plus any modifiers appropriate to the situation. This effect is not cumulative with any other effect that grants you an extra attack when making a full attack, such as the Rapid Shot feat, a speed weapon, or the haste spell.

You must wear bracers of quick strike for 24 hours before you can access their abilities. If you take them off, they become inactive until worn for an additional 24 hours.

Counterstrike Bracers

Aura faint psychokinesis; ML 7th
Slot wrists; Price 2,500 gp; Weight 1 lb.

Description Each of these steel bracers bears the image of a stacked pair of daggers pointed in opposite directions. Twice per day as an immediate action, counterstrike bracers allow you to answer an unsuccessful melee attack against you with an unexpected riposte. You can activate the bracers immediately after an enemy misses you with a melee attack. Doing this grants you a free attack of opportunity against that enemy (even if you have already used up your allotted attacks of opportunity for the round).

Mask of Mental Armor

Aura moderate abjuration; CL 9th
Slot head; Price 4,000 gp; Weight —

Description Broad lines radiate from the eyes of this blue leather mask, each ending in a spiral. This mask grants its wearer a +3 resistance bonus on saving throws against mind-affecting spells and abilities.

Psionatrix

Aura moderate (varies); ML 8th
Slot neck; Price 8,000 gp; Weight —

Description This heavy bronze amulet features a pair of wings that cover the upper chest. At the pendant’s center rests a glimmering, multifaceted crystal.

Each discipline of psionics has its own psionatrix. Wearing one adds a +1 enhancement bonus to the save DC of powers of the appropriate discipline. For example, the DC to resist compelling voice is 1 higher when the caster wears a psionatrix of telepathy.

The glow in the heart of the central crystal varies depending on its school: light blue (clairsentience), green (metacreativity), red (psychokinesis), violet (psychometabolism), orange (psychoportation), and yellow (telepathy).

Shadow Cloak

Aura strong conjuration and illusion; CL 12th
Slot shoulders; Price 5,500 gp; Weight 1 lb.

Description This black cloak was fashioned by drow, and writhes as if it were alive. A shadow cloak grants a +1 deflection bonus to AC. In addition, three times per day when you are attacked, you can use the cloak as an immediate action to produce one of the following effects: you can gain concealment for 1 round, or you can teleport to a space you can see clearly up to 10 feet in any direction.

Talisman, Githborn

Aura moderate transmutation; ML 7th
Slot neck; Price 1,800 gp; Weight —

Description A swirl of carved arabesques marks the top of this otherwise austere gray stone pendant. Both githyanki knights who captain astral longships and githzerai monks who hunt in rrakmas are known to wear these stones, which are designed for use against their most hated enemies.

Three times per day as an immediate action, the wearer of a githborn talisman can gain a +2 insight bonus on attack rolls and weapon damage rolls against aberrations, as well as a +2 insight bonus on Will saves made against the extraordinary, supernatural, and spell-like abilities of aberrations. This benefit lasts for 1 round.

Third Eye, Freedom

Aura moderate psychoportation; ML 7th
Slot eyes; Price 2,600 gp; Weight —

Description Once per day as an immediate action, the wearer of this third eye can gain the benefits of the slip the bonds power for 1 round.

Third Eye, Surge

Aura strong clairsentience; ML 13th
Slot eyes; Price 2,100 gp; Weight —

Description A third eye surge has 3 charges per day. Spending 1 or more charges as a swift action grants you an insight bonus on Strength checks, Dexterity checks, Strength- and Dexterity-based skill checks, and weapon damage rolls for 1 round.
     1 charge: +2 insight bonus.
     2 charges: +3 insight bonus.
     3 charges: +4 insight bonus.
