SPECIAL ABILITIES

Dominate (Su)

Ctenmiir can crush a humanoid opponent's will as a standard action. Anyone Ctenmiir targets must succeed on a DC 18 Will save or fall instantly under Ctenmiir's influence, as though by a dominate person spell (CL 12th). The ability has a range of 30 feet.

Blood Drain (Su)

Ctenmiir can suck blood from a grappled opponent; if Ctenmiir establishes or maintains a pin, he drains blood, dealing 1d4 points of Constitution damage. Ctenmiir heals 5 hit points or gains 5 temporary hit points for 1 hour (up to a maximum number of temporary hit points equal to his full normal hit points) each round he drains blood.

Children of the Night (Su)

Once per day, Ctenmiir can call forth 1d6+1 rat swarms, 1d4+1 bat swarms, or 2d6 giant spiders as a standard action. These creatures arrive in 2d6 rounds and serve Ctenmiir for up to 1 hour.

Change Shape (Su)

Ctenmiir can change use change shape to one of the following shapes as a standard action.  He can remain in animal form indefinitely.

  • dire bat: Large size (Reach 5 ft.), −4 AC, +4 Str, −2 Dex, fly 40 ft. (good),
  • monstrous spider: −3 AC, +2 Str, climb 30 ft., tremorsense 30 ft.

Gaseous Form (Su)

As a standard action, Ctenmiir can assume gaseous form (CL 5th), but he can remain gaseous indefinitely and has a fly speed of 20 ft. with perfect maneuverability.

Spider Climb (Ex)

Ctenmiir can climb sheer surfaces as though under the effects of a spider climb spell.

Create Spawn (Su)

Ctenmiir can create spawn out of those it slays with blood drain or energy drain, provided that the slain creature is of humanoid type. The victim rises from death as a vampire spawn in 1d4 days. This vampire is under Ctenmiir's command, and remains enslaved until Ctenmiir's destruction. Ctenmiir may have enslaved spawn totaling no more than 12 Hit Dice; any spawn Ctenmiir creates that would exceed this limit become free-willed undead. Ctenmiir may free an enslaved spawn in order to enslave a new spawn, but once freed, a vampire or vampire spawn cannot be enslaved again.

Shadowless (Ex)

Ctenmiir casts no shadows and shows no reflection in a mirror.
