Whelm

Slot none; CL 20th; Weight 5 lbs.; Aura strong abjuration, transmutation, divination, and evocation

Alignment LN; Senses 120 ft., darkvision; Int 15, Wis 12, Cha 15; Ego 18

Communication empathy, telepathy, speech (Dwarf, Giant, Goblin)

DESCRIPTION

This +3 returning warhammer can be thrown with a range increment of 50 feet. It was forged by dwarves for the purpose of killing giants, trolls, and goblinoids, and the hammer glows blue whenever such foes are within 60 feet. It can also sense hidden gold and gems within 60 feet, and the wielder can pinpoint such objects as though using a detect magic spell.

Once per day, the wielder of Whelm can strike the ground to send out a powerful shockwave.  Each creature of the wielder's choice who is on the ground and within 60 feet must make a DC 17 Fortitude save or be stunned for 1d4 rounds.  This is the equivalent of a 5th-level spell.

Whelm is a lawful weapon, so characters who are not lawful gain one negative level when holding or wielding the item (see intelligent item alignment).
