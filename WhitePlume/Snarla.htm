SPECIAL ABILITIES

Arcane Bond (Sp)

Once per day, Snarla can use her ring to cast any one spell that is in her spellbook and she is capable of casting, even if the spell is not prepared. This spell is treated like any other spell cast by Snarla, including casting time, duration, and other effects dependent on the Snarla’s level. This spell cannot be modified by metamagic feats or other abilities. 

Change Shape (Su)

Snarla can change shape between three forms—her human form, her dire wolf form, and her hybrid form. Because of her quick change spell, she can shift to her hybrid form as a swift action, and if she attacks a creature that same round it is denied its Dexterity bonus to AC against the first attack.

Her equipment melds into the new form when she changes to a dire wolf, but not when she changes to hybrid form.  In any case, she reverts to human form when slain.

Note that Snarla's Constitution score increases in her hybrid form, giving her 35 extra hit points.
