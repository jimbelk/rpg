Wave

Slot none; CL 20th; Weight 4 lbs.; Aura strong abjuration, divination, enchantment, evocation, and transmutation

Alignment N; Senses 120 ft., darkvision; Int 14, Wis 10, Cha 18; Ego 20

Communication empathy, telepathy, speech (Common, Aquan, and all other underwater languages)

DESCRIPTION

This +3 trident is an exquisite weapon engraved with images of waves, shells, and sea creatures. Its special powers function only for a neutral wielder who worships the god Poseidon, though any creature who picks up the weapon may choose to immediately convert to a neutral alignment and the worship of Poseidon. Any creature not of neutral alignment who holds the trident gains two negative levels as long as the item is in hand (see intelligent item alignment).

For an appropriate wielder, Wave functions as both a trident of fish command and a trident of warning. It also has the properties of a pearl of the sirines and can be used as a cube of force, though the latter powers are activated through command words instead of by pressing the faces of a cube. Finally, whenever the trident scores a critical hit against a living creature, it immediately dehydrates the target, dealing 10d6 extra points of damage.
