Blackrazor

Slot none; CL 20th; Weight 8 lbs.; Aura strong abjuration, divination, and necromancy

Alignment CN; Senses 120 ft., darkvision; Int 17, Wis 10, Cha 19; Ego 16

Communication empathy, telepathy, speech (Common)

DESCRIPTION

This +3 greatsword shines like a piece of night sky filled with stars. Its black scabbard is decorated with pieces of cut obsidian.

Whenever you reduce a conscious foe to 0 or fewer hit points with this weapon, the target immediately dies and you gain temporary hit points equal to the maximum hit points of the target. These hit points fade after 24 hours. As long as these temporary hit points last and you keep Blackrazor in hand you gain a +4 morale bonus to attack rolls, saving throws, skill checks, and ability checks.

In addition, the wielder of Blackrazor can detect living creatures of size Tiny or larger within 60 feet. This functions as a detect evil spell, except that all living creatures emit an aura as though they were evil.

Blackrazor is a chaotic weapon, so characters who are not chaotic gain one negative level when holding or wielding the item (see intelligent item alignment).
