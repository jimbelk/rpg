Some NPC's

Dyllon Ungart

Dyllon is a duergar paladin who is a member of the Unseen, a secret organization of good and neutral followers of St. Cuthbert who combat mind flayers and aboleths and guard the world against extraplanar incursions from hell, the abyss, and the far realm.  Members of the Unseen also participate in more mundane heroic adventures, such as fighting derro and gibberlings, slaying powerful demons or evil dragons, and so forth.

Dyllon is based in the duergar city of Gracklstugh, where he poses as a travelling merchant who dabbles in adventuring.  In addition to more mundane roles, he is always on the lookout for the arrival of the Lightbringers prophesied by the Air Weird.  According to the Weird, the Lightbringers will be four mysterious travellers in Gracklstugh who can perceive the future and are destined to fill the world with heaven's light.  Dyllon believes this prophecy may refer the four party members.

Like you, Dyllon is currently staying at the Cold Foundry Inn in Gracklstugh. You have adventured with him several times in the last month, though you didn't understand his true nature until your most recent adventure.  He seems to have good contacts in Gracklstugh, including Gartokkar Firehand (see below) and a former captain of the Stone Guard named Idwal Coalhewer. You don't know much about Dyllon's background, but he did tell Jaeden a story about his experience in a gigantic Lowerworld rift known as the Maddening Dark.

Dyllon is tall for a duergar, and his skin has a bit more color than most, owing to his urdunnir grandfather.  Dyllon, Lavor, and Jaeden are currently under arrest by the Stone Guard and locked in Overlake Hold.

 

Thikka

Thikka is a ratfolk alchemist who lives in Gracklstugh and is good friends with Dyllon.  Like him, Thikka is a member of the Unseen.  Dyllon introduced you to her some time ago, and at the time she seemed unusually interested in human culture.

Thikka was attacked by the followers of Yog-Sothoth while you were on your most recent adventure.  A baleful polymorph spell turned her into a mundane rat, but Morgana managed to reverse the effects using a dispel magic.  Morgana and Thikka are currently hiding in a permanent rope trick safehouse in Gracklstugh created by the Unseen.

 

Zov

Zov is drow vampire and a member of the cult of Yog-Sothoth.  He casts divine spells including fleshworm infestation and black tentacles (both 4th level spells), and his drow shadow cloak makes it difficult to attack him physically.

Zov participated in the attack on Dyllon at the Cold Foundry Inn, together with the medusa Euryale (see below) and the avolakia Shuy-Nil (now dead).  Forced into gaseous form, he escaped into the whorlstone tunnels underneath the city, where his coffin is presumably located.

Zov later appeared to Jaeden in a dream using some form of magical communication.  He claims to be a former member of the Unseen who abandoned the organization after becoming a vampire.  He argues that the world is a terrible place full of evil and suffering, and that the only rational response is to work towards its end.  He also claims that the party members are the "scions of Yog-Sothoth", and have been sent here by the elder evil to destroy the world.

 

Euryale

Euryale is a medusa witch and a member of the cult of Yog-Sothoth.  She has access to 5th-level spells such as baleful polymorph and also the ice tomb major hex (usually only available to witches of 10th level and higher).

Euryale participated in the attack on Dyllon at the Cold Foundry Inn, together with the drow vampire Zov (see above) and the avolakia Shuy-Nil (now dead). She ran upstairs near the end of the battle and then somehow disappeared—Lavor was unable to find her on the second floor despite no obvious exits.

According to Zov, Euryale is "quite taken" with Jaeden, whatever that means.

 

Gartokkar Firehand

Gartokkar Firehand is the head of the Firehand clan, one of the main smithing clans in Gracklstugh.  The Firehands are renowned for their armor, but they also sell weapons and other goods. The Firehand clan wields significant political power in the city, and are universally feared.

Gartokkar hired the party to rescue his youngest son Thar Firehand after Thar and his company were ambushed by derro near Murgol's Hollow. The party later found Thar dead but were able to save his son Ivar as well as recover the bodies of Thar and his wife Ulara.

Thaluul

Thaluuls is the head enforcer for the Firehand clan, and reports directly to Gartokkar.  He has a reputation for both intelligence and cruelty, and he is rumored to be heavily involved in the decision-making for the Firehand business.

Thaluul is apparently an expert in languages and was surprised to hear the party speaking a language that he does not know.  He has since asked Jaeden to tutor him in Common, and offered to free him from the duergar prison for that purpose.

 

Gengir Ironhead

Gengir is an officer in the Stone Guard who works as a spy for the mind flayers. He arrested Dyllon, Lavor, and Jaeden after the battle with the cult of Yog-Sothoth and interrogated them aggressively, in an apparent abuse of his authority.

Dyllon discovered some time ago that Gengir was compromised by the mind flayers and shared this information with the leadership of the guard. Rather than arrest him, the decision was made to keep Gengir in place and monitor his activities.
