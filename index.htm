Rulings

Wrist Sheaths

In addition to the listed items, a wrist sheath or spring-loaded wrist sheath can also be used to store one potion, scroll, dorje, or similar item.

Liberation Domain Powers

The Liberation (Su) power of the Liberation domain does not require an action to begin (see this thread), but the Freedom's Call (Su) power requires a standard action to activate.  The latter continues automatically each round (no action required), and can be ended as a free action at the beginning of Neblen's turn.  If Neblen is knocked unconscious or otherwise disabled while this ability is active, it continues automatically until all of its rounds are expended.

The Suffocation Spell

A creature who fails its initial save against this spell is staggered for the duration of the spell, and is unable to speak or cast spells with verbal components during this time. Each round at the beginning of its turn (including the first round after the spell is cast), the creature must make another saving throw or advance one step on the suffocation track.  The first such failed save causes a creature to drop to 0 hp and fall unconscious.  A second such failed save causes the creature to drop to -1 hit points and start dying, and a third such failed save causes the target to die.  A creature whose hit points have dropped to 0 or less because of this spell cannot be magically healed while the spell is ongoing.

The Extend Spell metamagic feat can be applied to suffocation, increasing the duration to six rounds.  This significantly increases the deadliness of the spell.

The Threefold Aspect Spell

A witch who memorizes and casts this spell at the beginning of every day and typically uses only one of the three forms can treat the ability score modifiers as permanent bonuses (i.e. similar to the effect of a headband of intellect).

House Rules

Copying Witch Spells

Other witches in this campaign world are happy to share witch spells, but they typcially charge a fee equal to (spell level squared) x (5 gp) for each spell.  Copying a spell requires a Spellcraft check (DC 15 + spell level), but you should feel free to take 10 on this check.

Used Wands

Partially used wands are available for sale at half the usual price.  Such a wand has 1d20+10 charges remaining.  Do not roll the number of charges until you are sure that you are buying the wand.

Shared Power

When using the Shared Power [Metapsionic] feat, the target is treated as having a Dexterity of 0.  The feat can also be used with a range of touch, which requires no attack roll.

Psionics

Many of the psionic races and psionic classes from Psionics Unleashed are common in this campaign world.  All of the common psionic items (many of which are usable by non-psionic characters) are available for sale at most major cities.

We are using psionics-magic transparency, except for some minor house rules involving psionic skills:

  • The Knowledge (psionics) skill can be used to identify psionic monsters, but such monsters can also be identified using an appropriate skill for their type.  For example, a psionic aberration can be identified using either Knowledge (psionics) or Knowledge (dungeoneering).
  • The Spellcraft skill can be used to identify powers and psionic effects, but non-psionic characters take a −4 penalty when using Spellcraft in this fashion.  Psionic characters do not take a corresponding penalty to Spellcraft when identifying magical spells or magical effects.
  • The Autohypnosis skill works normally.
  • The Use Magic Device skill can be used to activate psionic devices, but non-psionic characters take a −4 penalty to the Use Magic Device check when using the skill in this fashion.

Flight

We are using the D&D version 3.5 rules for movement in three dimensions and tactical aerial movement.  The Fly skill does not exist.

Hit Points

Do not roll for hit points.  All PC's have the average number of hit points for their level, rounding down.

Disallowed Spells

The blood money spell is disallowed.

Spellcraft

Here are some additional uses for the Spellcraft skill.

Table: Spellcraft DCs
Task Spellcraft DC
Modify a spell (minor alteration) 15 + double spell level
Modify a spell (typical alteration) 20 + double spell level
Modify a spell (major alteration) 25 + double spell level

Modify a Spell

You can use Spellcraft as a free action to alter a spell that you cast in a minor way.  For example, you could:

  • Use the grease spell to create flammable grease,
  • Create a particularly complicated illusion with silent image,
  • Cast a targeted spell when you barely have line of sight, or
  • Aim a particularly difficult lightning bolt.

This effect may even apply to an ongoing spell, but it must be a spell that you yourself cast (including a spell from a wand or a scroll).
