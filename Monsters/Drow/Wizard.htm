SPECIAL ABILITIES

Shadow Cloak (Su)

Three times per day when a drow wizard is attacked, he can use his shadow cloak as an immediate action to produce one of the following effects: he can gain concealment for 1 round, or he can teleport to a space he can see clearly up to 10 feet in any direction.

Touch of Darkness (Sp)

As a melee touch attack, a drow priestess can cause a creature’s vision to be fraught with shadows and darkness for 5 rounds. The creature touched treats all other creatures as if they had concealment, suffering a 20% miss chance on all attack rolls.

Eyes of Darkness (Su)

For 5 rounds each day, a drow priestess's vision is not impaired by lighting conditions, even in absolute darkness and magic darkness. These rounds do not need to be consecutive.

Command Spiders (Su)

You can use your channel negative energy to command spiders within 30 ft.  This works just like the Command Undead feat, but applies to spiders instead of undead.
