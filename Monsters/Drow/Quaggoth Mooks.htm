Ferocity (Ex)

A quaggoth mook remains conscious and can continue fighting even if its hit point total is below 0. The quaggoth is still staggered and loses 1 hit point each round. The quaggoth still dies when its hit point total reaches a negative amount equal to its Constitution score.
