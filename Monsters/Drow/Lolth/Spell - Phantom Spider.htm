Phantom Spider

School conjuration (creation; Level spider 3

CASTING

Casting Time 10 minutes

Components V, S

EFFECT

Range 0 ft.

Effect one quasi-real, spider-like creature

Duration 1 hour/level (D)

Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance no

DESCRIPTION

You conjure a Large, quasi-real, spider-like creature. It can be ridden only by you or by the one person for whom you specifically created the mount. A phantom spider has a black body and smoke-colored, insubstantial legs that make no sound. It does not fight, but animals shun it and refuse to attack it.

The mount is AC 19 (-1 size, +7 natural armor, +3 Dex) and 7 hit points + 1 hit point per caster level. If it loses all its hit points, the phantom spider disappears. A phantom spider has a speed of 20 feet per two caster levels, to a maximum of 100 feet at 10th level. It can bear its rider's weight plus up to 10 pounds per caster level.

These mounts gain certain powers according to caster level. A mount's abilities include those of mounts of lower caster levels.

8th Level: The mount can use spider climb at will (as the spell, no action required to activate this ability).

10th Level: The mount gains a burrow speed of 20 ft.  It can burrow through loose soil and earth, but not solid rock. Its rider can breathe without difficulty while the mount is burrowing.

12th Level: The mount can use air walk at will (as the spell, no action required to activate this ability) for up to 1 round at a time, after which it falls to the ground.

14th Level: The mount can fly at its speed with a bonus on Fly skill checks equal to your caster level.

Phantom Spider, Communal

Level spider 4

Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)

Effect up to six quasi-real, spider-like cratures

DESCRIPTION

This spell functions like phantom spider, except you can summon up to six spiders, and you divide the duration in 1-hour intervals among the spiders summoned.
