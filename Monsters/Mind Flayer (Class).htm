Class Features

The following are class features of the contemplative.

Alien Physiology

Because of their alien physiology, many magic items and spells used by PC's do not have any effect on mind flayer.  In particular, mind flayers are not affected by any items or spells that grant an enhancement bonus to an ability score, an enhancement bonus to natural armor, a deflection bonus to armor class, or a resistance bonus to saving throws.

Psi-Like Abilities

All mind flayers can use vigor, defensive precognition, offensive precognition, and inertial armor at will as psi-like abilities, with a manifester level equal to their racial hit dice.  These abilities are usually already active when the mind flayer is first encountered.

Ability Scores

Mind flayer ability scores increase with their challenge rating, as shown in Table: Mind Flayer.  Mind flayers use Intelligence to manifest powers and cast spells, but their psi-like abilities are based on Charisma.

Armor Class

A mind flayer receives no natural armor bonus, but typically have active bonuses from defensive precognition and inertial armor, as well as a bonus from high Dexterity.

Basic Traits

All mind flayers have the following traits:

  • Darkvision 60 ft.

  • Spell resistance equal to 17 + challenge rating.

  • Telepathy with a range of 100 ft.

  • Four natural tentacle attacks with a base damage of 1d4.  These tentacles have the grab ability, and as such mind flayers receive a +4 bonus on combat maneuver checks made to start and maintain a grapple.  Note that the Weapon Finesse feat applies to these tentacle attacks.

Mind Blast (Ps)

This psionic attack is a cone 60 feet long. Anyone caught in this cone must succeed on a Will save (DC 1/2 the mind flayer's racial hit dice + the mind flayer's Cha modifier) or be stunned for 3d4 rounds. This ability is the equivalent of a 4th-level psionic power.

Extract (Ex)

A mind flayer that begins its turn grappling a pinned, stunned, or helpless creature can extract that creature's brain with a successful grapple check.  This grapple check automatically succeeds if the creature is helpless, but not if it is stunned (though stunned creatures lose their Dexterity bonus to CMD and take an additional -4 penalty to CMD). A creature whose brain is extracted dies instantly. This power is useless against constructs, elementals,
oozes, plants, and undead. It is not instantly fatal to foes with multiple heads, such as ettins and hydras.

Spells or Powers

A mind flayer can either manifest powers or cast arcane spells as a caster, with a manifester level or caster level equal to the creature's challenge rating. Some common mind flayer powers and spells are listed below.

Feats

All mind flayers take Combat Casting, Improved Initiative, Spell Penetration, Toughness, and Weapon Finesse as their first five feats.

Skills

Mind flayers receive 4 + Int modifier skills per Hit Die.  Class skills for a mind flayer include Acrobatics, Climb, Escape Artist, Intimidate, Knowledge (any one), Perception, Spellcraft, Stealth, Survival, and Swim, as well as the class skills for whichever spellcasting class the mind flayer casts as.

Most mind flayers take maximum ranks in Intimidate, Perception, and Spellcraft, as well as at least one Knowledge skill.  Other popular skills include  Bluff, Diplomacy, Escape Artist, Sense Motive, Stealth, and Use Magic Device.

Gear

The total gear value carried by a mind flayer is shown in Table: Mind Flayer.  Mind flayers use their own items that are not available elsewhere, and such items cannot usually be bought or sold by members of other races.

Power Gems

A power gem is a use-activated magic item similar in function to a potion, except that power gems can hold powers as well as spells, and have no restriction on the type of power or spell used. A power gem must be held in hand to be used, and are activated by a command thought.  As with a potion, the bearer of a power gem is both the caster and the target of the spell or power. The caster level (or manifester level) of a power gem is always at least 8, and power gems up to CL 18 (or ML 18) are quite common.

Summoning Beads

A summoning bead is a single-use item that summons a single creature, as with the summon monster spell.  A mind flayer can use the bead by drawing it and throwing it against the ground (a standard action).  The summoned creature remains for 2 minutes (20 rounds).

Ring Gate

Aura strong conjuration; CL 17th
Slot —; Price 7,500 gp; Weight 1 lb.

Description This black ring is inscribed with mysterious runes.  On command, the ring opens an interdimensional connection to another plane, similar to the spell gate.  All nearby creatures are sucked into the gate, though they can attempt a Reflex save (DC 23) to escape.  The save DC decreases by 2 for every 5 ft. of distance to the gate. Airborn creatures receive a -4 penalty to their saves, while creatures who can grab hold of something secure receive a +4 bonus. Prone creatures also recieve a +4 bonus to their saves.  The gate remains open for 1d4 rounds, and then disappears abruptly, consuming the ring in the process.

Cephalometer

Aura moderate enchantment and divination; CL 9th
Slot —; Price 36,450 gp; Weight 1 lb.

Description This psionically empowered caliper measures a creature’s head size via two inwardly curved, iron projections. These projections are connected at one end with a bolt that allows them to open or close. In addition, the caliper wielder can use dominate person and feeblemind (DC 17) each once per day on any willing or subdued creature whose head is between the projections. Fitting the calipers to a subject’s head provokes an attack of opportunity.

In addition to the two powers noted above, a cephalometer applied to the head of a willing or subdued subject can conduct a mind probe once per day for up to 10 minutes. All the subject’s memories and knowledge are accessible, from memories deep below the surface to those that are easily called to mind. The wielder can telepathically pose one question, and the caliper extracts the answer from the subject’s brain and relays it directly to the wielder’s mind. These answers are to the best of the subject’s
knowledge. The wielder and the subject do not need to speak the same
language, though less intelligent creatures may yield only appropriate visual
images in answer to questions. The cephalometer may be used to probe a
sleeping subject, though the subject may attempt a DC 17 Will save to
wake after each question. Creaturesprotected by mind blank or those that are not affected by mind-affecting effects are immune to this mind probing.

Tentacle Extension

Aura strong evocation and trasmutation; CL 12th
Slot —; Price 32,000 gp; Weight 2 lb.

Description This item appears as a flexible, 5-foot length of iron composed of many thin iron threads. A razor-sharp blade is attached to the end, and a small duct runs throughout the length of the extension to convey an illithid’s natural flesh-dissolving enzymes to the tip.

A tentacle extension grants a tentacled wearer a +2 enhancement bonus on attack and damage rolls made for that tentacle and increases its base damage die by one step. For illithids, this means that a tentacle attack with a tentacle extension deals base damage of 1d6 instead of 1d4 points. A tentacle extension increases its wearer’s reach by 5 feet. A creature with multiple tentacles can wear multiple extensions, if desired.
