Lightning Lance

Aura faint evocation; CL 3rd; Slot —; Price 3,800 gp

DESCRIPTION

This 3-foot-long silver tube is a magical weapon fashioned by the grell.  Five times per day, a grell who wields it can shoot a bolt of electricity at a single target within 60 feet, dealing 3d6 points of damage with a successful ranged touch attack. This item only functions for grell, but other creatures can attempt to activate it with Use Magic Device.

CONSTRUCTION REQUIREMENTS

Grell Alchemy, shocking grasp; Cost 1,900 gp
