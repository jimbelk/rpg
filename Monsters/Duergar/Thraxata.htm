SPECIAL ABILITIES

Smoke Vision (Ex)

Thraxata can see perfectly in smoky conditions (such as those created by pyrotechnics).

Frightful Presence (Ex)

Thraxata's very presence unsettling to foes. Whenever she attacks, all opponents within 150 ft. who can see her and have 14 HD or fewer must make a DC 19 Fortitude save or become shaken for 5d6 rounds (or panicked if the opponent has 4 HD or fewer). Activating this ability is a free action, and the save DC is Charisma-based. An opponent that succeeds on the saving throw is immune to Thraxata's frightful presence for 24 hours. Frightful presence is a mind-affecting fear effect.

Flight (Ex)

Thraxata can fly with a speed of 200 ft. and poor maneuverability.  This means:

  • She cannot hover.
  • If she does not fly at least 100 ft. in a round she must land at the end of her movement.
  • She can turn at most 45° per 5 ft. of movement.
  • It can ascend at most 5 ft. per 5 ft. of movement. Squares in the upwards direction cost double movement.
  • It can descend at most 5 ft. per 5 ft. of movement.  Squares in the downwards direction cost half movement.
  • She must fly at least 10 ft. straight between going down and going up.

Thraxata can use her Flyby Attack feat to take a move action and another standard action at any point during the move.

Vulnerability to Cold (Ex)

Thraxata takes half again as much damage (+50%) from cold, regardless of whether a saving throw is allowed or if the save is a success or failure. 

Breath Weapon (Ex)

Thraxata can exhale a 50 ft. cone of fire (in a 90° arc) as a standard action once every 1d4 rounds, dealing 10d10 points of fire damage (Reflex DC 24 half). A breath weapon always starts at an intersection adjacent to Thraxata and extends in a direction of her choice. Thraxata can use her breath weapon even when she is grappling or being grappled.  The save DC is Constitution-based.

DESCRIPTION

Thraxata is a 72 year old female red dragon and the current mate of Themberchaud.  She is 55 ft. long, has a maximum wingspan of 75 ft., and weighs 10 tons.
