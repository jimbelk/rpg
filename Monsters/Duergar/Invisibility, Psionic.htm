Invisibility, Psionic

Discipline psychometabolism

MANIFESTING

Display olfactory

Manifesting Time 1 standard action

EFFECT

Range Personal

Target You

Duration 1 min./level (D)

Power Points 3

DESCRIPTION

This power makes you invisible, replicating the effects of an invisibility spell.

Augment. You can augment this power in any one of the following ways.

1. If you spend 2 additional power points, this power's duration is 10 min./level rather than 1 min./level.

2. If you spend 4 additional power points, this power's duration is 1 hour/level rather than 1 min./level.

3. If you spend 4 additional power points, this power functions as the greater invisibility spell, with a duration of 1 round/level.
