SPECIAL ABILITIES

Vicious Attack (Ex)

An empty scabbard killer takes 1d6 points of damage each time he successfully strikes a foe.

Dissolving Weapon (Ex)

The first time each day that each of an empty scabbard killer's weapons strikes a foe it deals an additional 6d6 acid damage.

Twin Strike (Ex)

An empty scabbard killer can make one attack with both of his weapons as a standard action or as an attack of opportunity.

Inertial Armor (Su)

An empty scabbard killer is surrounded by an invisible field of energy that grants a +8 armor bonus.

Chameleon (Su)

An empty scabbard killer's skin skin and equipment take on the color and texture of nearby objects, including floors and walls, granting a +10 enhancement bonus to Stealth.

Third Eye of Freedom (Su)

Once per day as an immediate action, an empty scabbard killer gains the benefits of the slip the bonds power for one round.

Ring of Escaping (Su)

Once per day as a swift action, an empty scabbard killer gains a fly speed of 60 feet with good maneuverability for 1 round.

TACTICS

An empty scabbard killer prepares for combat by manifesting vigor, skate, and force screen, granting 35 temporary hit points, a +15 ft. enhancement bonus to speed, and a +4 shield bonus to AC for 7 minutes.

An empty scabbard killer can use inevitable strike once per day as a standard action and once per day as a swift action. The first grants a +20 bonus to the killer's next attack roll, and the second grants a +17 bonus to the killer's next attack roll.
