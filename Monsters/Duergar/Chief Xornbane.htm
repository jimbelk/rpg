SPECIAL ABILITIES

Speed (Su)

Three times per day as a swift action, Chief Xornbane can produce a haste effect on himself (as the haste spell) that lasts for 1 round. 

Mindarmor (Su)

Three times per day as an immediate action, Chief Xornbane can gain a +5 bonus on Will saves to resist mind-affecting spells and abilities until the start of his next turn.

Blinding Strike (Su)

Three times per day as a swift action, Chief Xornbane can make one additional attack at his highest base attack bonus as part of a full attack action. This effect is not cumulative with any other effect that grants an extra attack when making a full attack, such as the haste spell.

Smiting (Su)

Twice per day as a swift action, Chief Xornbane can imbue all of his attacks with radiant energy for a short time. Until the beginning of the Chief's next turn, all of the Chief's melee attacks deal double damage. During this time, Chief Xornbane sheds light similar to that of a lightstone of illumination.

Healing (Su)

Once per day as a swift action, Chief Xornbane can heal 4d8+7 hit points worth of damage.

Flight (Su)

Once per day as a standard action, Chief Xornbane can speak a command word to affect himself with a fly spell for 5 minutes. During this time, the Chief sheds light similar to that of a lightstone of illumination.

Freedom (Su)

Once per day as an immediate action, Chief Xornbane can gain the benefits of the slip the bonds power for 1 round.

Piledriver (Ex)

As a standard action, Chief Xornbane can make a single melee attack with a two-handed weapon. If the attack hits, he may make a bull rush or trip combat maneuver against the target of his attack as a free action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity.

Pushing Assault (Ex)

When Chief Xornbane hits a creature of his size or smaller with a two-handed weapon attack modified by the Power Attack feat, he can choose to push the target 5 feet directly away from him instead of dealing the extra damage from Power Attack. If he scored a critical hit, he can instead push the target 10 feet directly away from him. This movement does not provoke attacks of opportunities, and the target must end this move in a safe space it can stand in.
