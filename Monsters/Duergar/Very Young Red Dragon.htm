SPECIAL ABILITIES

Smoke Vision (Ex)

A very young red dragon can see perfectly in smoky conditions (such as those created by pyrotechnics).

Flight (Ex)

A very young red dragon can fly with a speed of 150 ft. and average maneuverability.  This means:

  • It cannot hover.
  • If it does not fly at least 75 ft. in a round it must land at the end of its movement.
  • It can turn at most 45° per 5 ft. of movement, or spend 5 ft. of movement to turn 45° in place (maximum 90° turn in any one space).
  • It can ascend at most 10 ft. per 5 ft. of forward movement. Squares in the upwards direction cost double movement.
  • It can descend at any angle. Squares in the downwards direction cost half movement.

The dragon can use its Flyby Attack feat to take a move action and another standard action at any point during the move.

Vulnerability to Cold (Ex)

Red dragons take half again as much damage (+50%) from cold, regardless of whether a saving throw is allowed or if the save is a success or failure. 

Breath Weapon (Ex)

A very young red dragon can exhale a 30 ft. cone of fire as a standard action once every 1d4 rounds, dealing 4d10 points of fire damage (Reflex DC 17 half). A breath weapon always starts at an intersection adjacent to the dragon and extends in a direction of its choice. A dragon can use its breath weapon even when it is grappling or being grappled.  The save DC is Constitution-based.

DESCRIPTION

These 13-year-old dragons are the spawn of Thraxata and Themberchaud.  Each of these dragons is 16 ft. long, has a maximum wingspan of 24 ft., and weighs 320 lbs.
