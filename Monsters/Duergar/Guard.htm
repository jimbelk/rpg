SPECIAL ABILITIES

Quick Strike (Su)

Once per day as a swift action, a duergar guard can make one extra attack with any weapon he is holding as part of a full attack action. This effect is not cumulative with any other effect that grants an extra attack when making a full attack such as the haste spell.

Mighty Blow (Su)

Once per day as a swift action, a duergar guard can make an attack with his greatsword that deals an extra 3d6 points of damage on a successful hit. The guard must declare that he is using this power before the attack roll is made.
