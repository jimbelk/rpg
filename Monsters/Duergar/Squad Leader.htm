SPECIAL ABILITIES

Blinding Strike (Su)

Three times per day as a swift action, a duergar squad leader can make one additional attack as part of a full attack action. This attack is made at the squad leader's full base attack bonus, plus any modifiers appropriate to the situation. This effect is not cumulative with any other effect that grants an extra attack when making a full attack, such as the haste spell.

Ring of Escaping (Su)

Once per day as a swift action, a duergar squad leader can gain a fly speed of 60 feet with good maneuverability for 1 round.
