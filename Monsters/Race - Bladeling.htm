Bladeling

Bladelings are xenophobic beings of humanoid shape. Though they hail from the Infernal Battlefield of Acheron (LN/LE), most scholars believe that the race emigrated there from another plane, possibly the Nine Hells of Baator, the Bleak Eternity of Gehenna, or even some unknown metal-based plane.

Physical Description: A bladeling has skin of a dull metallic hue, spotted with patches of metallic spines. Its eyes gleam like shards of purple ice, and its blood is black and oily.

Society: Most bladelings live in the city of Zoronor, on Ocanthus, the fourth layer of Acheron. Some, however, wander the other layers of Acheron or even the other planes.

Bladelings are superstitious and xenophobic creatures, so it's no surprise that they abhor intrusions into their home territory. (Of course, as residents of Ocanthus—a place of flying storms of blades—they rarely have visitors.) Their society is prone to internal strife, but they immediately band together against external dangers. Bladelings that travel the planes are reasonably amiable toward strangers, perhaps because they have picked up a degree of tolerance for other beings through constant contact. Even so, they tend not to trust anyone they do not know.

Alignment and Religion: Bladelings are always lawful, and most bladelings are lawful neutral or lawful evil.  Most bladelings worship Hextor.

Adventurers: Most bladeling adventurers are fighters or cleric/fighters, and the race is known for its assassins as
well.

Racial Traits

  • Ability Score Modifiers: +2 Dexterity
  • Type: Outsider (extraplanar)
  • Size: Medium
  • Base Speed: 30 feet
  • Languages: Undercommon and Infernal.

Defense Racial Traits

  • Natural Armor: A bladeling's metallic skin gives it a +4 natural armor bonus.
  • Resistance: Bladelings have cold resistance 5 and fire resistance 5.
  • Immunities: Bladelings are immune to acid and take no damage from rust attacks, despite their metallic skin.
  • Damage Reduction: Bladelings have DR 5/(magic or bludgeoning)

Offense Racial Traits

  • Claw: A bladeling can attack with its claw as a primary natural weapon, dealing 1d6 damage.
  • Razor Storm (Ex): Once per day, a bladeling can expel shrapnellike bits of its skin in a 15-foot cone, dealing 2d6 points of piercing damage to any creature in the area. A Reflex save (DC 10) halves the damage. After this attack, the bladeling’s natural armor bonus drops to +2 for 24 hours.  The save DC is Constitution-based.

Senses Racial Traits

  • Darkvision: Bladelings have darkvision 60 feet.

Challenge Rating

  • A bladeling with levels of a PC class has a challenge rating equal to his or her class level.
