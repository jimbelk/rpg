Grimlock Ring

Aura faint evocation; CL 3rd; Slot —; Price 6,500 gp

DESCRIPTION

This plain metal ring was crafted by the mind flayers. It grants a +1 deflection bonus to AC, a +1 enhancement bonus to natural armor, and a +1 resistance bonus on all saving throws.

CONSTRUCTION REQUIREMENTS

Feats Forge Ring; Powers fortify, mental barrier, minor metamorphosis; Cost 3,250 gp
