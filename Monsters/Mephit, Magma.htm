SPECIAL ABILITIES

Pyrotechnics (Sp)

One fire source within 640 ft. emits one of the following effects.  If the mephit uses itself as a fire source, it takes 6 points of damage.

  • Fireworks: All creatures within 120 ft. of the fire source must make a DC 14 Will save or be blinded for 1d4+1 rounds.
  • Smoke Cloud:  20-ft. spread; lasts 6 rounds; blocks all sight, including darkvision; all creatures within must make a DC 14 Fortitude save or take a −4 penalty to Strength and Dexterity for 1d4+2 rounds.

Flight (Ex)

A magma mephit fly with a speed of 40 ft. and average maneuverability.  This means:

  • It cannot hover.
  • If it does not fly at least 20 ft. in a round it must land at the end of its movement.
  • It can turn at most 45° per 5 ft. of movement, or spend 5 ft. of movement to turn 45° in place (maximum 90° turn in any one space).
  • It can ascend at most 10 ft. per 5 ft. of forward movement. Squares in the upwards direction cost double movement.
  • It can descend at any angle. Squares in the downwards direction cost half movement.

Vulnerability to Cold (Ex)

Magma mephits take half again as much damage (+50%) from cold, regardless of whether a saving throw is allowed or if the save is a success or failure.
