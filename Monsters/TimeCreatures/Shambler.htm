Time Shambler

Time Shambler CR 5

XP 1,600

CE Medium aberration

Init +7; Senses darkvision 60 ft., scent; Perception +18

Aura horrific appearance (Will DC 15)

DEFENSE

AC 21, touch 13, flat-footed 18 (+3 Dex, +8 natural)

hp 54 (4d8+36)

Defensive Abilities regeneration 10 (positive energy)

Fort +12, Ref +7, Will +4

Immune most effects that require a Fortitude or Will save

OFFENSE

Speed 60 ft.

Melee bite +10 (2d10+9 plus madness)

Special Attacks psychic scream (Will DC 15)

STATISTICS

Str 23, Dex 17, Con 27, Int 6, Wis 16, Cha 18

Base Atk +3; CMB +9; CMD 22

Feats Improved Initiative, Toughness

Skills none; Languages none

SPECIAL ABILITIES

Horrific Appearance (Su) - DC 15

Any creature who sees a time shambler must make a DC 15 Will save or take 1d4 points of Wisdom damage.  Whether the target succeeds or fails at this save, they are then immune to this effect for 24 hours.

Madness (Su) - DC 15

Creatures bitten by a time shambler must make a DC 15 Will save or take 1d6 points of Wisdom damage.  Anyone reduced to 0 Wisdom in this way is affected as if by a confusion spell until they regain at least one point of Wisdom.

Psychic Scream (Su) - 4d6, range 30 ft., Will DC 15 half

As a standard action, a time shambler can let out a scream that deals 4d6 psychic damage to all creatures (except time shamblers) within 30 feet (Will DC 15 half).
