Pincer Staff

Cost 20 gp Weight 15 lbs.

Damage 1d8 (small) 1d10 (medium) Critical ×2 Type bludgeoning

Category two-handed Proficiency exotic

Weapon group polearms

Special grapple, reach

Kuo-toa fighters and whips often employ this two-handed exotic weapon. If you hit an opponent within one size category of yourself with a pincer staff, you may attempt to start a grapple as a free action without provoking an attack of opportunity. Make a combat maneuver check to start the grapple (without the –4 penalty for not having two hands free); success means you and the target are grappled, and you can perform a move or damage grapple action against him on subsequent rounds.

Kuo-toa treat the pincer staff as a martial weapon rather than an exotic weapon, so all Kuo-Toa fighters are automatically proficient with it. Kuo-toa clerics (whips) can also use it, since it is the favored weapon of the kuo-toa deity Blibdoolpoolp.
