Troll, Crystalline

This big, bipedal creature is about twice as tall as a human. It has long, ungainly arms and legs. Its hide glimmers faintly, reflecting and refracting light.

Troll, Crystalline CR 7

XP 3,200

CN Large humanoid (giant)

Init +6; Senses darkvision 60 ft., low-light vision, scent; Perception +12

DEFENSE

AC 18, touch 11, flat-footed 16 (+2 Dex, +7 natural, -1 size)

hp 105 (10d8+60); regeneration 5 (sonic)

Fort +13, Ref +5, Will +4

Immune acid

Weaknesses vulnerable to sonic

OFFENSE

Speed 30 ft.

Melee 2 claws +12 (1d6+6) and bite +7 (1d6+3)

Space 10 ft.; Reach 10 ft.

Special Attacks rend (2 claws, 1d6+9)

STATISTICS

Str 23, Dex 14, Con 23, Int 6, Wis 9, Cha 8

Base Atk +7; CMB +14; CMD 26

Feats Improved Initiative, Iron Will, Power Attack, Skill Focus (Perception), Vital Strike

Skills Perception +12

Languages Giant

TACTICS

During Combat Like an ordinary troll, a crystalline troll has no fear of death and joins combat with vigor and pleasure. It attacks the nearest opponent.

 

Morale Even when being hurt with sonic attacks, the troll will continue its assault.

ECOLOGY

Environment any underground

Organization solitary or gang (2–5)

Treasure standard

More charismatic than an ordinary troll, a crystalline troll is still only slightly less feral. A crystalline troll’s skin is made of a glasslike material, making the creature look like a troll covered in rock crystal.

Crystalline trolls stand more upright than ordinary trolls. An adult is 11 feet tall and weighs 600 pounds.
