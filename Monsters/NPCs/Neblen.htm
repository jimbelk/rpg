SPECIAL ABILITIES

Quick Runner's Shirt - 1/day

Once per day as a swift action, Neblen can take an additional move action to move and then immediately end his turn, losing any unspent actions.

Agile Feet (Su) - 8/day

As a free action, Neblen can gain increased mobility for 1 round. For the next round, Neblen ignores all difficult terrain and does not take any penalties for moving through it.

Liberation (Su) - 10 rounds/day

Neblen has the ability to ignore impediments to his mobility. For 10 rounds/day, Neblen can move normally regardless of magical effects that impede movement, as if he were affected by freedom of movement. This effect occurs automatically as soon as it applies. These rounds do not need to be consecutive, and this power is a free action to activate.

Dimensional Hop (Sp) - 100 feet/day

Neblen can teleport up to 100 feet per day as a move action. This teleportation must be used in 5-foot increments and such movement does not provoke attacks of opportunity. Neblen must have line of sight to his destination to use this ability. He can bring other willing creatures with him, but he must expend an equal amount of distance for each creature brought. 

Freedom's Call (Su) - 10 rounds/day

Neblen can emit a 30-foot aura of freedom for 10 rounds per day. Allies within this aura are not affected by the confused, grappled, frightened, panicked, paralyzed, pinned, or shaken conditions. This aura only suppresses these effects, and they return once a creature leaves the aura or when the aura ends, if applicable. These rounds do not need to be consecutive.  This power is a standard action to activate, and can be ended at any time as a free action.

Channel Positive Energy (Su) - 5d6, 5/day

Neblen can release a wave of positive energy that heals 5d6 damage for all creatures within 30 feet.  This is a standard action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity.

Quick Channel - Neblen can channel energy as a move action by using two daily uses of that ability.

Selective Channeling - Neblen can choose to exclude up to 2 targets from his channel energy.
