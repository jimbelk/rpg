SPECIAL ABILITIES

Metamagic Adept (Ex)

Twice per day, Narri can apply any one metamagic feat she knows to a spell she casts without increasing the casting time. She must still expend a higher-level spell slot to cast this spell.

Bloodline Arcana

Whenever Narri applies a metamagic feat to a spell that increases the slot used by at least one level, the spell's DC increases by +1. This bonus does not stack with itself and does not apply to spells modified by the Heighten Spell feat.
