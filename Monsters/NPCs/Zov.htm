SPECIAL ABILITIES

Shadow Cloak (Su)

Three times per day when Zov is attacked, he can use his shadow cloak as an immediate action to produce one of the following effects: he can gain concealment for 1 round, or he can teleport to a space he can see clearly up to 10 feet in any direction.

Dominate (Su)

Zov can crush a humanoid opponent's will as a standard action. Anyone Zov targets must succeed on a DC 23 Will save or fall instantly under Zov's influence, as though by a dominate person spell (CL 12th). The ability has a range of 30 feet.

Gift of Madness (Su)

Zov can tap into the unthinkable void between the stars and cause a single living creature within 30 feet to become confused for 8 rounds. A successful Will save (DC 21) negates the effect. This is a mind-affecting compulsion effect. He can use this ability 10 times per day.

Blood Drain (Su)

Zov can suck blood from a grappled opponent; if Zov establishes or maintains a pin, he drains blood, dealing 1d4 points of Constitution damage. Zov heals 5 hit points or gains 5 temporary hit points for 1 hour (up to a maximum number of temporary hit points equal to hi full normal hit points) each round he drains blood.

Children of the Night (Su)

Once per day, Zov can call forth 1d6+1 rat swarms, 1d4+1 bat swarms, or 2d6 giant spiders as a standard action. These creatures arrive in 2d6 rounds and serve Zov for up to 1 hour.

Change Shape (Su)

Zov can change use change shape to one of the following shapes as a standard action.  He can remain in animal form indefinitely.

  • dire bat: Large size (Reach 5 ft.), −4 AC, +4 Str, −2 Dex, fly 40 ft. (good),
  • monstrous spider: −3 AC, +2 Str, climb 30 ft., tremorsense 30 ft.

Gaseous Form (Su)

As a standard action, Zov can assume gaseous form (CL 5th), but he can remain gaseous indefinitely and has a fly speed of 20 ft. with perfect maneuverability.

Spider Climb (Ex)

Zov can climb sheer surfaces as though under the effects of a spider climb spell.

Wings of Darkness (Su)

As a swift action, Zov can manifest a set of translucent, inky wings that grants him a fly speed of 60 feet with good maneuverability. He can use these wings for 8 minutes per day. This duration does not need to be consecutive, but it must be spent in 1-minute increments.

Create Spawn (Su)

Zov can create spawn out of those it slays with blood drain or energy drain, provided that the slain creature is of humanoid type. The victim rises from death as a vampire spawn in 1d4 days. This vampire is under Zov's command, and remains enslaved until Zov's destruction. Zov may have enslaved spawn totaling no more than 16 Hit Dice; any spawn Zov creates that would exceed this limit become free-willed undead. Zov may free an enslaved spawn in order to enslave a new spawn, but once freed, a vampire or vampire spawn cannot be enslaved again.

Shadowless (Ex)

Zov casts no shadows and shows no reflection in a mirror.

Cloak of Darkness (Su)

Zoc can conjure a cloak of shadowy darkness that grants him a +6 armor bonus and a +4 circumstance bonus on Stealth checks (both already included in the statistics). He can use this cloak for 8 hours per day. The duration does not need to be consecutive, but it must be spent in 1-hour increments.
