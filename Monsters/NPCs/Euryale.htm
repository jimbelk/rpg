SPECIAL ABILITIES

Petrifying Gaze (Su)

Gaze attack—Turn to stone permanently, 30 feet, Fortitude DC 20 negates. The save DC is Charisma-based.

Accursed Hex (Ex)

When a target of Euryale's slumber, misfortune, or ice tomb hexes makes their saving throw, Euryale can attempt to try the same hex again on the following round. If the second attempt fails Euryale cannot target the same creature with that hex again for 1 day.

Toxic Words (Su)

Whenever Euryale targets a single creature with a hex, she can imbue her hex with a dose of poison as a swift action:

Toxic Words Poison: save Fort DC 22; frequency 1/round for 6 rounds; effect 1d3 Str; cure 2 consecutive saves.

If the creature fails its save against the hex, the creature must succeed at a Fortitude save against the poison or become poisoned.
