SPECIAL ABILITIES

Aura of Courage and Resolve (Su)

Each ally within 10 feet of Dyllon gains a +4 morale bonus on saving throws against fear and charm effects. This ability functions only while Dyllon is conscious.

Lay on Hands (Su) – 9/day

Dyllon can use his lay on hands ability in the following ways:

  • As a swift action, Dyllon can heal himself 5d6 points of damage. In addition, this alleviates the fatigued and staggered conditions and acts as a neutralize poison (CL 11th).
  • As a standard action, Dyllon can heal a living creature he touches 5d6 points of damage. In addition, this alleviates the fatigued and staggered conditions and acts as a neutralize poison (CL 11th).
  • As a standard action, Dyllon can deal 5d6 points of damage to an undead creature with a successful melee touch attack (+15 melee).
  • As a standard action, Dyllon activates a nimbus of light that lasts for 1 minute.  All allies within 30 feet of Dyllon gain a +1 morale bonus to AC, attack rolls, damage rolls, and saving throws against fear. In addition, allies in Dyllon's nimbus immediately heal 1d4 points of ability damage as the spell lesser restoration, though each creature can only be healed in this way once per day.
  • (2 charges) As a standard action, Dyllon channels positive energy as an 11th-level cleric, either healing 6d6 points of damage for all living creatures within 30 feet, or dealing 6d6 damage to all undead creatures in that radius (Will DC 17 half).
  • (2 charges) As a free action, Dyllon's aura of justice grants Dyllon's smite evil ability to all allies within 10 feet. Such allies gain 1 charge of smite evil which must be used before the start of Dyllon's next turn, and whose benefits last for only 1 minute.

Smite Evil (Su) – 4/day

As a swift action, Dyllon chooses one target within sight to smite. If this target is evil, he gains a +2 to attack rolls and +11 to damage rolls against the target, as well as a +2 bonus to AC against attacks made by the target. Smite evil attacks automatically bypass any DR the target might possess. These bonuses remain for the rest of the day or until the target is dead.

If the target is an undead creature, an outsider with the evil subtype, of an evil-aligned dragon, the bonus to damage on Dyllon's first successful attack increases to +22.

Detect Evil (Sp)

Dyllon can use detect evil at will. Alternatively, Dyllon can concentrate on a single item or individual within 60 feet as a move action and determine if it is evil, learning the strength of its aura as if having studied it for 3 rounds.

Divine Bond (Sp)

Once per day as a standard action, Dyllon can enhance his weapon by calling upon the aid of a celestial spirit for 11 minutes. When called, the spirit causes the weapon to shed light as a torch, and grants the weapon an additional +3 enhancement bonus.

Dyllon can use part of this bonus to add one or more of the following weapon properties: axiomatic (+2), defending (+1), disruption (+2), flaming (+1), flaming burst (+2), holy (+2), keen (+1), merciful (+1), and speed (+3).
