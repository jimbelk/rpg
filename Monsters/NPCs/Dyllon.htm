SPECIAL ABILITIES

Lay on Hands (Su) – 9/day

Dyllon can use his lay on hands ability in the following ways:

  • Heals Himself: swift action; Dyllon heals 5d6 plus mercies
  • Heal Another: standard action; creature touched heals 5d6 plus mercies
  • Damage Undead: standard action; melee touch +15; 5d6 damage (no save)
  • Nimbus of Light: standard action; radius 30 feet; lasts 1 minute; allies gain a +1 morale bonus to AC, attack rolls, damage rolls, and saves against fear. Also, allies immediately heal 1d4 ability damage as per the spell lesser restoration, though each creature can only be healed once per day in this way.
  • Channel Energy: 2 charges; standard action; radius 30 ft.; heal 6d6 damage or deal 6d6 damage to undead (Will DC 17 half).
  • Aura of Justice: 2 charges; free action; radius 30 feet; before Dyllon's next turn, each ally in the area can smite evil on one creature; bonuses are the same as Dyllon's and last for 1 minute.

Smite Evil (Su) – 4/day

As a swift action, Dyllon chooses one target within sight to smite. If this target is evil, he gains the following:

  •   +2 to attack rolls against the target
  •   +11 to all damage rolls against the target,
  •   +2 to AC against attacks made by the target, and
  •   Dyllon's attacks automatically bypass the target's DR.

These bonuses remain for the rest of the day or until the target is dead.

If the target is undead, an outsider with the evil subtype, or an evil dragon, the bonus to damage on Dyllon's first successful attack increases to +22.

Divine Bond (Sp) – 1/day

As a standard action, Dyllon enhances his weapon with the aid of a celestial spirit for 11 minutes. This causes the weapon to shed light as a torch, and grants an additional +3 enhancement bonus.  All or part of this bonus can be used for one or more of the following weapon properties: axiomatic (+2), defending (+1), disruption (+2), flaming (+1), flaming burst (+2), holy (+2), keen (+1), merciful (+1), and speed (+3).
