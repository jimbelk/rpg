Quaggoths

Quaggoths are a race of monstrous humanoids that resemble albino cave bears. Primitive and simple, they are forced into slavery by other races—especially the drow.

Physical Description: A quaggoth is a humanoid with beastlike features, having a head something like that of a bear. A typical male quaggoth stands just over 6 feet tall and weighs 250 pounds or more. Female quaggoths are slightly smaller, but no lighter. They stand about 5-1/2 feet tall and have the same build as males, though they tend to obesity. Regardless of gender, every quaggoth has thick white fur over a densely muscular body, though some quaggoths dye or paint their fur black to camouflage themselves better.

Quaggoth Society: Though quaggoths are numerous, many serve the drow as slaves. These poor souls suffer terribly under pitiless overseers who work them to death or expend them in large numbers for the interminable contests between houses.

Those that manage to avoid the slavers form clans that can include up to fifty members. On rare occasions, several clans might meet in a massive cavern to hold a gathering. Such convocations occur after a series of damaging attacks on quaggoth holdings or if some other danger threatens them as a whole.

Among these savages are two general outlooks. Most quaggoths abide by the old customs and "follow the beast." They are extremely violent and reckless, seeing no point in using weaponry or making any advances to their society. Such quaggoths are virtually indistinguishable in nature and outlook from wild animals. The other quaggoths "follow magic." This expression is something of a misnomer, since spellcasting among the quaggoths is extremely rare. Instead, these individuals improve upon their innate strengths, which they see as a gift from their gods (though still primitive and elemental). They revel in the power of the weapons they wield, treating them as mystical relics, and seek out magic items of all kinds. They mark their choice by dying their white fur with dung, blood, and black ichor. Those who follow magic are the prominent members of their clans. They are generally more intelligent, usually becoming war leaders, and don't throw their lives away recklessly. 

Quaggoth Tribes: Quaggoths can be found anywhere, but they prefer the Middleworld and Lowerworld to the Upperworld. A quaggoth community is filthy, fouled by droppings, piles of bones, rotting meat, and rubbish heaped against the walls. Quaggoth lairs are rife with disease, and many clans are weakened by outbreaks of filth fever.

Feral bands of quaggoths haunt many of the lonelier portions of the world, existing as roving bands of marauders who prey on anything they can catch. Bloodthirsty and savage, quaggoths can descend into a screaming animal frenzy at the slightest provocation, losing any semblance of reason until they destroy (or are destroyed by) whatever foe they happen to encounter.

Religion: Quaggoth clerics are extremely rare and are usually devoted to demons or gods of trickery and death.

Alignment: Quaggoths are usually neutral evil. They are self-interested beings, with little understanding of or concern for the consequences of their actions, motivated purely to fulfill their every desire.

Adventurers: Like minotaurs, quaggoths are frequently found as slaves and warriors in the cities of more civilized folk. Quaggoth mercenaries can find work almost anywhere. While few actually become adventurers, some have risen above the bloodlust and senseless violence of their kin to become the stalwart companions of adventuring parties. Good quaggoths often pit themselves against the tyranny of those evil races that hold their kinfolk in slavery.

Racial Traits

  • Ability Score Modifiers: Quaggoths are physically powerful but slow and primitive. They gain +6 Strength, +2 Constitution, −2 Dexterity, and −2 Intelligence.
  • Type: Quaggoths are monstrous humanoids.
  • Size: Quaggoths are Medium creatures and thus receive no bonuses or penalties due to their size.
  • Base Speed: Quaggoths have a base speed of 30 feet and a climb speed of 30 feet.
  • Languages: Quaggoths begin play speaking Undercommon. Quaggoths with high Intelligence scores can choose from the following languages: Abyssal, Aklo, Dwarven, Elven, Giant, Goblin, or Orc

Defense Racial Traits

  • Natural Armor: Quaggoths have a +5 natural armor bonus.
  • Immunities: Quaggoths are immune to poison and fear effects.

Feats and Skills Racial Traits

  • Stealthy: Quaggoths gain a +4 racial bonus on Stealth checks.

Senses Racial Traits

  • Superior Darkvision: Quaggoths have superior darkvision, allowing them to see perfectly in the dark up to 120 feet.
  • Scent: Quaggoths have the scent special ability.

Offense Racial Traits

  • Ferocity: Quaggoths possess the ferocity ability which allows them to remain conscious and continue fighting even if their hit point totals fall below 0. Quaggoths are still staggered at 0 hit points or lower and lose 1 hit point each round as normal.
  • Natural Attacks: Quaggoths have 2 claw attacks (1d4), which are primary natural attacks, and one bite (1d6), which is a secondary natural attack.

Weakness Racial Traits

  • Light Sensitivity: Quaggoths are dazzled in areas of bright light.
