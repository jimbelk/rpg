Crystal of Power Knowledge

Aura strong metacreativity; ML 17th; Weight –
Slot none; Price 1,000 gp (1st), 4,000 gp (2nd), 9,000 gp (3rd), 16,000 gp (4th), 25,000 gp (5th), 36,000 gp (6th), 49,000 gp (7th), 64,000 gp (8th), 81,000 gp (9th)

Description This large crystal contains the knowledge of a single psionic power (chosen by the creator when the item is crafted). If the bearer is a manifester and has that power on her class power list, she may use her power points to manifest that power as if it were one of her powers known.

Construction Requirements Craft Psionic Item, creator must be able to manifest the power contained in the stone; Cost 500 gp (1st), 2,000 gp (2nd), 4,500 gp (3rd), 8,000 gp (4th), 12,500 gp (5th), 18,000 gp (6th), 24,000 gp (7th), 32,000 gp (8th), 40,500 gp (9th)

Ring of Power Knowledge

Aura moderate or strong (no discipline); ML 7th
Slot ring; Price 1,500 gp (Type I), 6,000 gp (Type II), 13,500 gp (Type III), 24,000 gp (Type IV); Weight –

Description This ring comes in four types: ring of power knowledge I, ring of power knowledge II, ring of power knowledge III, and ring of power knowledge IV. All of them are useful only to manifesters.

Through study, the wearer can gain the knowledge of a single power in addition to those allotted by her class and level. A ring of power knowledge I can hold 1st-level powers only, a ring of power knowledge II 1st- or 2nd-level powers, a ring of power knowledge III powers of 3rd level or lower, and a ring of power knowledge IV up to 4th-level powers.

A ring of power knowledge is only a storage space; the wearer must still either address a power stone of the power or encounter an active or manifested version of the power and succeed at a DC 20 Spellcraft check to teach the power to the ring. Thereafter, the manifester may manifest the power as though she knew the power and it appeared on her class's power list.

Powers that do not appear on the wearer's class list are treated as one level higher for all purposes (storage and manifesting).

Construction Requirements Craft Psionic Item, creator must be able to manifest powers of the power level to be granted; Cost 750 gp (Type I), 3,000 gp (Type II), 6,750 (Type III), 12,000 gp (Type IV).

Rods, Metapsionic

Metapsionic rods hold the essence of a metapsionic feat, allowing the user to apply metapsionic effects to powers (but not psi-like abilities) as they are manifested. Each rod allows the wielder to apply the metapsionic effect to a manifested power three times per day. Using a metapsionic rod does not change the manifesting time or the number of power points required to manifest the power, but it does require the rod's wielder to expend her psionic focus.

All the metapsionic rods described here are use-activated (but manifesting powers in a threatened area still draws an attack of opportunity). A manifester may only use one metapsionic rod on any given power, but it is permissible to combine a metapsionic rod with metapsionic feat possessed by the rod's wielder (though this usually requires expending two psionic foci). In this case, only the feats possessed by the wielder adjust the power points required to manifest the power.

Lesser and Greater Metapsionic rods: A manifester wielding a lesser metapsionic rod cannot expend more than 7 power points on the power affected by the rod.  This increases to 13 power points for a normal metapsionic rod, or 19 power points for a greater metapsionic rod.

Vestment, Power Thieving

Aura strong telepathy; ML 17th
Slot body; Price 5,000 gp; Weight 1 lb.

Description The surface of this delicate-looking blue silk robe is adorned with tiny embossed runes across its entire surface. If the wearer is a manifester, once per day she may use her power points to manifest a power from a power stone as if she knew that power. The power must be on her power list, and the manifester must have addressed the stone before she can use it in this fashion. Activating the vestment is not an action, but manifesting the power otherwise works as normal, including manifesting time, augmentation, and so on. Using a power thieving vestment's properties does not consume the power stone.

Construction Requirements Craft Psionic Item, thieving mindlink; Cost 2,500 gp.
