Earth Weird Prophecy

The end has come.

Those below prepare, for they have seen the void.

Those above search in vain, for what can they do?

Not even the gods can control it.

And far in the distance, beyond the astral sea,

Beyond the measure of miles and aeons.

They laugh.

The game has seven pieces, but none know their purpose.

Three in the depths, preparing, building.

And four above, wandering the shadows, gaining power.

The board has been set, the light extinguished.

The game is played in the dark. Always the dark.

But the rules are strange, with pieces moving forwards and back.

The start the same as the end. The end.

Of course, this world will perish.

It is obvious.

Once the game ends, the board is eaten.

That's what they do. They eat games.

Not long now. The pieces are almost in place.

Let's begin.
