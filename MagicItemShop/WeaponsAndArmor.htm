Rules (Read First)

The shop has an enormous amount of weapons and armor.  Instead of providing a long list of weapons and armor, I've only listed the most useful items below.  Before scrolling down, your character or characters must search through the weapons and armor as follows:

1 character:  It takes 3 minutes for one character plus Belwar to search through these items.  Please time 3 minutes before scrolling down.

2 characters:  It takes 2 minutes for two characters plus Belwar to search through these items.  Please time 2 minutes before scrolling down.

3 characters:  It takes 90 seconds for all three characters plus Belwar to search through the items.  You do not need to pause in this case, but 90 seconds will be deducted from your allotted time.
