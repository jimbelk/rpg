Firm: IntSecIntSec (Internal Security)

Internal Security is a combination of Homeland Security, riot police and narc squad. Not only does The Computer keep tabs on everything with omnipresent cameras and recording devices, it also maintains Internal Security spies as another check on the loyalty of its citizens. Many IntSec agents are planted in the other service groups. They have informants everywhere.

In addition to ratting on traitors, IntSec agents run ‘sting’ operations designed to tempt citizens into treason. Because promotion within IntSec is based on the number and prestige of traitors unmasked, IntSec agents are notorious for rooting out treason that isn’t even there to begin with.

Of course, false accusations of treason are themselves treason. However, if an IntSec agent and a regular citizen mutually accuse each other of treason, all else being equal, The Computer is more likely to believe the agent.
