Hygiene Officer

Only the most trusted servants of The Computer serve as hygiene officer. Yours are the duties of The Scrubbing Helmet, that legendary bastion of clean corridors and well-flossed Troubleshooters. Serve his memory well!

Your responsibilities include monitoring the PHL (Personal Hygiene Level) of each team member, reporting when a Troubleshooter fails his PHT (Personal Hygiene Test), and implementing ESP (Emergency Sanitation Procedures) against any and all offenders.

To aid you, The Computer has generously provided you a PHTCMK (Personal Hygiene Test and Cleanliness Maintenance Kit) for performing PHTs and ESPs on your fellow Troubleshooters.

Take your duty seriously. Recent team leader post-mission reports indicate 52% of all mission disasters can be blamed on hygiene neglect by the hygiene officer. Failure to report sub-standard PHLs is treason.

Remember, ACTIAMT: A Clean Team Is A Mean Team!

How to Perform a PHT

A PHT is an intensive physical inspection used to determine if a citizen's PHL meets the MCL (Minimum Cleanliness Level). Any citizen that fails a PHT must undergo an immediate ESP.

The steps of the PHT are at your discretion, and may include a CBI (Complete Body Inspection), lectures on citizen hygiene, and so forth. However, every PHT must include taking an SCS (Skin Core Sample). Your PHTCMK includes an SCS-6 (Skin Core Sampler Type 6), which has been developed by safety-conscious R&D engineers to take an SCS quickly and painlessly.

The SCS-6 takes minute tissue samples and determines their chemical structure. It will recommend ESPs if there is too much dirt in the tissue sample. To use the SCS-6, simply wrap it around an arm or leg of the citizen undergoing the PHT and turn it on. Then just wait a few seconds and the machine will tell you if ESPs are indicated.

Initiating ESPs

You have the following options for initiating ESPs:

  1. Place a priority call to CCC (Citizen Cleanliness Central). They will immediately deploy a CCB (Citizen Cleanliness Bot) who can handle the ESPs for you in a timely fashion.
  2. In emergency situations where the response time of a CCB may be inadequate, simply spray the offender with his choice of overall body cleansers from your PHTCMK. CCC recommends the New Perma Scent.
  3. If further ESPs seem pertinent, administer the experimental Clean-It-Up pill to any unhygenic citizens.

Organizing ECCs

You have the right to form ECCs (Emergency Cleaning Crews) to combat Commie filth and general untidiness. Whenever the team encounters an area of Alpha Complex that does not meet the MCL, you may temporarily assume command of the team until every last bit of trash is deposited in a proper receptacle. Your ECC can be made up of any citizens in the area, or members of your Troubleshooter team.

Enforcing PHM

The first step in the never-ending fight against Commie dirt is continuous PHM (Preventive Hygiene Maintenance) by all members of your team. PHM is a regimen of grooming procedures that you must enforce during the mission. Your PHTCMK includes some special equipment for performing PHM:
