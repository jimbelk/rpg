Bot Normalizers vehicle therapists have ‘downtime’ sessions with bot brain autopilots experiencing stress on the job. They also bring AI's from vehicles that have been abused and ‘ripped’ (that is, have had parts ripped off or crushed for whatever reason) back into functional society. Most AI brains in severely damaged vehicles are still fully functional and simply need to be transplanted to a new vehicle. AI's which have been irrevocably damaged or traumatized are removed from their housing and destroyed, but this is very rare because vehicle CPUs are so valuable.
